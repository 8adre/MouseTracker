MouseTracker

A mouse tracker for analyse the user behavior

Usage

  • You can drag'n'drop this scriptlet on your favorite bar of your browser

    MouseTracker

  • Go to a "simple" test page
  • Start a record by pressing the "r" key
  • Move, click, etc... but please don't quit this page :-)
  • When you have finish press "s" key to save your records
  • You can replay your record by press "p" key and on the popin click on your last record

Limitations

  • Only tested with lastest Chrome (Web SQL / Canvas)
  • by scriptlet it's not possible to folow a user between two pages
  • it's a Alpha version
